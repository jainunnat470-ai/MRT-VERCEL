Why Hallmark Matters

Without official hallmarking, it is impossible to verify silver purity by eye. Hallmarking protects you from adulterated silver alloyed with cheap, toxic materials.

🏛️

Government Certified

Every piece is certified by Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) recognized assay centers, representing independent third-party quality assurances.

💎

925 Pure Sterling Silver

Our hallmark guarantees exactly 92.5% pure fine silver. The remaining 7.5% is alloyed copper, added to give silver the strength required for daily wear.
